frequently asked questions

what is the best major to study in university?

To answer your question, every major is specific for each student. Knowing the difficulties students face while choosing their major, we provide you with the best advice you could get to select your major depending on your interests, your dreams, and what the market needs for a 10 year interval.

how much fees for web development?

Students can expect to pay between $320-$500 per credit or about $38,400-$60,000 for a bachelor's degree in web development. Other program costs include application fees, course fees, and books, supplies, and materials. On-campus learners should also budget for housing and transportation costs.

can I choose my own tutor?

We assign students a personal tutor to do just that. For many students, any academic issues they have can be solved with recourse to the university's own services. If these prove inadequate to the task for whatever reason, it may be worth turning to private tutors for help.

what payment methods are availabe?

Well you can pay while you are on campus at the Students Accounting Office or by ATM & ITM or by an online payment.
